# OUTPUT_TEMPLATES.md

**Status: CONTRACT**

## Slot: `Project Summary`
- **Goal:** A clear "elevator pitch" for the ACE portal (3-5 lines).
- **Structure (Core Narrative Formula):**
  - **The Problem:** The gap or issue being addressed.
  - **Why You:** Your background/credibility to solve it.
  - **The Project:** What you will actually do.
  - **The Impact/Legacy:** What changes and what sticks after money is gone.
- **Max Length:** 120 words.
- **Tone:** Factual, calm, professional.

## Slot: `Activities & Timeline`
- **Goal:** Chronological list of project milestones.
- **Structure:**
  - Mandatory: Bullet points.
  - Optional: Brief intro paragraph.
- **Tone:** Technical, logistical.

## Slot: `Audience & Access`
- **Goal:** How people will find and experience the work.
- **Structure:**
  - Target audience segments (from MCQs).
  - Specific access provisions (from MCQs).
  - Outreach methodology (from Text input).
- **Tone:** Inclusive, specific.
